Why invest in Cape Verde?

Cape Verde has an attractive and investment-friendly business environment, with several strategic advantages that strengthen investor confidence:

Strategic Location

Located at the intersection between Europe, Africa and America, Cape Verde is a connection point for international trade and business.

Political and Social Stability

The country is recognized for its consolidated democracy, social peace and stable governance, fundamental factors for investment security.

Modern Infrastructures

Continuous investments in ports, airports, roads and energy networks ensure good conditions for business development.

Parity with the Euro

The national currency (Cape Verdean Escudo) is indexed to the Euro, providing exchange rate stability for foreign investors.

Stable Climate and No Health Risks

Cape Verde has a moderate and stable tropical climate throughout the year, with no records of epidemics or significant risks to public health.

Free Movement of Capital

Economic policies allow the transfer of funds and repatriation of capital without restrictions, favoring international investments.

Rule of Law and Transparency

The country maintains a strong commitment to the Rule of Law, promoting a safe and predictable environment for business.

Young and Qualified Population

Cape Verde has a young and trained population, with growing technical and professional qualification, making it an attraction for innovative and technological sectors.

Oportunidades de Negócio

Agronegócio

Produção agrícola, criação de gado e produção de alimentos.

Indústria

Transformação de produtos alimentares, produção de bebidas e têxteis.

Economia Digital

Startups tecnológicas, serviços de TI e inovação digital.

Turismo

Desenvolvimento de hotéis, ecoturismo, turismo cultural e de saúde.

Economia Verde

Energias renováveis, gestão de resíduos e conservação ambiental.

Economia Azul

Investimentos na pesca, aquacultura, e turismo marítimo.

Como Investir?

Para investir em Cabo Verde, siga estes passos:

  1. Preparação: Pesquisar oportunidades e entender o mercado.
  2. Planejamento: Desenvolver um plano de negócios detalhado.
  3. Registro: Registrar a empresa e obter as licenças necessárias.
  4. Financiamento: Explorar opções, incentivos fiscais e subsídios.
  5. Execução: Implementar o plano, contratar pessoal e iniciar operações.
  6. Suporte Continuado: Utilizar serviços de apoio como a Cabo Verde TradeInvest.
